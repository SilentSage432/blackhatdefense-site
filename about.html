About Black Hat Defense LLC

Black Hat Defense LLC is a cybersecurity consulting firm focused on ethical offensive security, adversarial risk assessment, and OSINT-driven exposure analysis. Our work is centered on helping organizations understand how they can actually be compromised—and what to do about it—before real attackers take advantage of those weaknesses.

We combine technical testing with intelligence-driven analysis to identify meaningful risk across applications, networks, infrastructure, and publicly exposed assets. All work is performed under controlled, authorized conditions and aligned to client objectives.

Our Philosophy

Effective security starts with realism, not assumptions. Many organizations focus inward while attackers begin externally—using publicly available information, misconfigurations, and unintended exposure to gain access or influence outcomes.

Our approach emphasizes:

  • Realistic testing aligned to real-world threat models
  • OSINT-driven visibility into external exposure and attack surface
  • Clear communication of risk and impact
  • Actionable remediation guidance, not noise
  • Respect for operational constraints and business priorities

The goal is not volume or theatrics, but decision-quality insight.

OSINT & Intelligence-Driven Assessment

Open-source intelligence (OSINT) plays a critical role in how modern attacks begin. We use OSINT techniques to identify publicly accessible information, assets, and exposure that may be leveraged as part of a broader attack path.

This includes analysis of:

  • Internet-facing systems and services
  • Unintended data exposure and metadata
  • Credential leaks and third-party platform exposure
  • Organizational footprint across public sources

OSINT is used to inform testing scope, improve realism, and help clients reduce unnecessary visibility and external risk.

Ethical and Authorized Testing

All work performed by Black Hat Defense LLC is conducted under explicit written authorization and within clearly defined legal and ethical boundaries. We do not engage in unauthorized testing, surveillance, or exploit development for malicious use.

Professionalism, discretion, and client trust are fundamental to how we operate.

Experience and Approach

Our experience spans modern IT environments, network infrastructure, industrial systems, and externally exposed organizational assets. Engagements are tailored to the client’s environment, risk tolerance, and operational realities.

We work collaboratively with stakeholders to ensure testing is effective, minimally disruptive, and produces results that can be acted on.
