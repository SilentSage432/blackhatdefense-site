Security Services

Black Hat Defense LLC provides authorized offensive security services designed to help organizations understand real-world risk, validate defensive controls, and prioritize remediation efforts.

All engagements are conducted with explicit authorization and clearly defined scope.

Penetration Testing

Penetration testing evaluates the security of systems, applications, and networks by simulating realistic attack scenarios under controlled conditions.

The goal is to identify exploitable weaknesses, assess potential impact, and provide clear, actionable remediation guidance.

  • External and internal network testing
  • Web application security testing
  • Authenticated and unauthenticated assessments
  • Clear reporting with prioritized findings

Adversarial Security Testing

Adversarial security testing goes beyond checklist-based assessments by focusing on how real attackers think, chain weaknesses, and achieve objectives.

These engagements are designed to test detection, response, and overall resilience rather than individual controls in isolation.

  • Threat-informed attack simulation
  • Control validation and gap analysis
  • Detection and response evaluation
  • Executive-level risk context

ICS / SCADA Security Assessments

Industrial control systems require a specialized approach that balances security testing with safety, availability, and operational constraints.

Assessments focus on identifying exposure, misconfigurations, and attack paths without disrupting critical processes.

  • ICS and SCADA architecture review
  • Network segmentation and exposure analysis
  • Protocol and device risk assessment
  • Operationally safe testing methodologies

OSINT & Attack Surface Analysis

Open-source intelligence (OSINT) analysis identifies publicly available information that may expose organizations to social engineering, reconnaissance, or targeted attacks.

This service helps organizations understand what an external adversary can learn without any direct access.

  • External asset discovery
  • Public exposure and data leakage identification
  • Brand and infrastructure reconnaissance
  • Risk-focused reporting

Engagement Model

All services are delivered under a formal agreement that defines scope, authorization, objectives, and reporting requirements.

Black Hat Defense LLC reserves the right to decline engagements that fall outside legal, ethical, or professional boundaries.
